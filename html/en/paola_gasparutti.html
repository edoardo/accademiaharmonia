Paola Gasparutti

PAOLA GASPARUTTI, was born in Cividale del Friuli (Udine) on July 6th 1961, in 1979 she graduated at the Istituto Magistrale “Orsoline” in Cividale del Friuli and in 2004 earned a second degree at the Istituto Socio Psico Pedagogico in San Pietro al Natisone attached to the convitto Nazionale Paolo Diacono of Cividale del Friuli.

In 2003 she was elected president of the Musical - Cultural Academy “HARMONIA”, a position she still holds.

Since 2000 she is member of A.D.I.F. (Associazione Docenti Italiani di Filosofia) of which she is the national secretary since September 2003.

Together with the president prof. Giuseppe Schiff, she has collaborated in the organization of the National Congress of Philosophy in 2003 (Filosofia & Arte – Cividale del Friuli), 2005 (...E dopo la vita? - Montesilvano – Pescara), 2007 (Verità del corpo – Cividale del Friuli), 2009 (Quale Etica per la Bioetica – Roma), 2011 (Democrazia, Verità, Pluralismo), 2013 (Domande filosofiche e relazioni educative).

In 2008 she collaborated with prof. Giuseppe Schiff, in the organization, in Cividale del Friuli, of the conference Il LOGOS in FRIULI VENEZIA GIULIA – Figure del pensiero filosofico friulano giuliano.
