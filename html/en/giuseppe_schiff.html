Giuseppe Schiff

GIUSEPPE SCHIFF was born in Porpetto (UD) in 1948, and since 1967 he has lead a number of choirs and orchestras.

Since 1988 he has conducted the choir of the Cultural - Musical Academy "HARMONIA", of which he is the artistic manager and the chief of the cultural activities too.

Vice - President of the A.D.I.F. (Association of Italian Philosophy Teachers), he organizes the biennial national conferences; he is Vice - Director of the review "For the Philosophy - Philosophy and Teaching".

He is a Philosophy, Psychology and Science of Education teacher at the Social - Psychological - Pedagogical and Linguistic High Schools of San Pietro al Natisone linked to the National Boarding School "Paolo Diacono".

He is in charge of Philosophical Anthropology and Philosophy of Knowledge at the High Institute of Religious Science of Udine.

He leads historical, philosophical and musical researches.
